Day 2 • Fri 26 Dec • City Highlights

Recommended Flow
10:30 Coffee/pastry near apartment
12:00 Duomo & Galleria (rooftops if clear)
13:30 Lunch (Luini / Obicà / Spontini)
14:30 Brera wander + Pinacoteca option
16:00 Sforza Castle → Parco Sempione → Arco della Pace
17:30 Duomo Christmas Market
19:00 Aperitivo (Brera / Corso Como / Navigli)
20:30 Dinner
22:30 Night walk or drinks (Navigli / Corso Como / gelato)

Breakfast / Coffee

• Panificio Pattini — classic Milanese pastries
• Marchesi 1824 — luxury pastries (Prada-owned)
• Cafezal (Brera) — specialty coffee
Post-Christmas mornings are calm and atmospheric.

Duomo & Galleria

• Duomo di Milano — do rooftops if weather is clear (amazing views).
• Galleria Vittorio Emanuele II — walk the arcade; mosaic bull spin for luck.
• Time: ~1–1.5 hours.

Lunch (Center)

• Luini (panzerotti, classic + cheap)
• Obicà Duomo (mozzarella bar, views)
• Spontini (iconic pizza slice)

Brera

• Wander Via Brera / Via Fiori Chiari; boutiques, galleries, cafés.
• Pinacoteca di Brera (optional quick visit).
• Gelato: Venchi or Rivareno.

Castle + Park

• Sforza Castle courtyards (free to walk).
• Continue to Parco Sempione → Arco della Pace (great photos).

Christmas Market

Duomo area Mercatini di Natale (late December still open): mulled wine, sweets, crafts, holiday music.

Aperitivo & Night

• Best areas: Brera (classy), Corso Como (modern), Navigli (party vibe).
• Picks: N’Ombra de Vin (Brera wine cellar), Deus Café (Corso Como), Spritz Navigli (cheap/fun).
• Late: Navigli walk, Corso Como skyline vibe, or gelato (Cioccolati Italiani / Grom).
Walking & Transport
• Steps: ~12,000–16,000 (easy/walkable).
• Metro: M1 red line to Duomo; to Navigli use tram 3/10 or M2 (Porta Genova).
• Most of the day is walkable between Duomo ↔ Brera ↔ Castle/Parco.
Keep this page handy offline; adjust times by ~1h later if you want a slower start.
