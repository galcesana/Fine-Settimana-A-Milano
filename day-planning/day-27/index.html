Day 3 • Sat 27 Dec • Lake Como & Brunate

Outline
08:30–09:30 Train Milan → Como
09:30–12:00 Como old town & lakefront
12:00–15:00 Funicular to Brunate, lunch, viewpoints
15:00–17:30 Back to Como • optional ferry/coffee
17:30–19:00 Aperitivo / early dinner in Como
Evening Return to Milan (many trains until ~22:00)

Trains to Como

• Routes: Milano Centrale → Como S. Giovanni or Milano Cadorna → Como Nord Lago.
• Tickets: ~€5–€12 one way (Trenord/Trenitalia/Trainline/Omio).
• Aim to arrive Como 09:30–10:00. Shift 1h later if needed.
• Buy online or at station; keep flexibility with one-way if you prefer.

Como Old Town (09:30–12:00)

  • Piazza Cavour on the water (coffee/pastry).
  • Lakefront promenade (lungolago) stroll.
  • Como Cathedral (Duomo) quick visit.
  • Piazza San Fedele cute square.
  • Walk toward Funicolare station (~10 min from Piazza Cavour).

Brunate (12:00–15:00)

• Funicolare Como–Brunate every ~15 min; ride ~7 min; return ticket ~€7–€14.
• Views at the top; stroll through village.
• Optional: Walk to Faro Voltiano lighthouse (30–40 min each way, check weather/daylight).
• Lunch in Brunate with lake views.
• Start heading back down by 14:30–15:00.

Afternoon Options (15:00–17:30)

• Winter ferry (reduced timetable) — short round-trip cruise or hop to Cernobbio.
• Alternatively: more promenade walking, hot chocolate, or Tempio Voltiano museum.

Aperitivo / Early Dinner (17:30–19:00)

Pick a lakeside bar/café for drinks + snacks or simple pasta/pizza/risotto before heading back.
Pre-trip Checklist
Day before: check weather (Como/Brunate), funicular maintenance, download ferry winter timetable, check/buy train times & prices.
Morning of: train tickets (or buy at station), funicular tickets, ferry ticket if cruising.
Trains back run frequently until ~22:00–22:30; aim for 19:00–20:00 or later if you’re enjoying.
