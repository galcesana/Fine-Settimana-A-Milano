Day 5 • Mon 29 Dec • City / Flex Day

Simple Outline
Day: Shopping / sightseeing (choose below)
Evening: Dinner & drinks

Shopping Routes

  • Quadrilatero (luxury): Via Montenapoleone, Via della Spiga, Via Manzoni, Corso Venezia.
  • Corso Vittorio Emanuele II: from Duomo toward San Babila (mid/high street).
  • Corso Buenos Aires: long high-street artery across budgets; finishes near Porta Venezia.
  • Via Torino: everyday shopping + corridor toward Navigli/Darsena via Colonne.

Neighborhoods to Pair

  • Porta Venezia: Liberty/Art Nouveau vibe; lively bars at night.
  • Chinatown (Via Paolo Sarpi): snacks, bubble tea, street food, then walk toward Sempione/Porta Nuova.
  • Corso Como → Gae Aulenti: aperitivo + skyline views.
  • Navigli/Darsena: canals for aperitivo/dinner/night atmosphere.

Food & Drinks Ideas

  • Aperitivo: Brera / Corso Como / Navigli.
  • Dinner: stay near where you shop (Quadrilatero → Brera; Buenos Aires → Porta Venezia; Via Torino → Navigli).
  • Sweet break: gelato (Cioccolati Italiani, Grom) or patisserie stops en route.
Keep it flexible; pick 1–2 shopping corridors + 1 neighborhood for aperitivo/dinner.
