Saturday 27/12/2025 • Lake Como + Brunate Day
Day Trip to Lake Como & Brunate
Chill day trip, not over-packed. You can shift everything 1 hour later if you prefer.
08:30–09:30 Train Milan → Como
Route: Milano Centrale → Como S. Giovanni or Milano Cadorna → Como Nord Lago
Ticket: ~€5–€12 one way (Trenord/Trenitalia/Trainline/Omio)
Aim to arrive in Como around 09:30–10:00
09:30–12:00 Como Old Town & Lakefront
• Piazza Cavour – main square on the water (coffee/pastry)
• Stroll the lungolago (lakefront promenade)
• Como Cathedral (Duomo) – quick visit
• Piazza San Fedele – cute square
• Walk towards Funicolare station (10 min from Piazza Cavour)
12:00–15:00 Funicular to Brunate + Lunch + Viewpoints
• Funicolare Como–Brunate: runs every 15 min, 7 min ride
• Return ticket: ~€7–€14
• Enjoy views at the top, walk through village
• Optional: Walk to Faro Voltiano lighthouse (30–40 min each way, check weather/daylight)
• Lunch in Brunate with lake views
• Start heading back down by 14:30–15:00
15:00–17:30 Back to Como + Optional Ferry Ride
• Winter ferry schedule (reduced timetable, check Navigazione Laghi)
• Options: Short round-trip cruise OR quick trip to Cernobbio
• Alternative: More promenade walking, hot chocolate, or Tempio Voltiano museum
17:30–19:00 Aperitivo / Early Dinner in Como
Bar for aperitivo (drinks + snacks) or simple pasta/pizza/risotto dinner
Evening Train Back to Milan
Many departures until ~22:00–22:30. Aim for 19:00–20:00 or later if having fun.

Transport To San Siro Stadium
Option 1 — Metro (Best and Easiest):
Total time: ~30–35 minutes

Step-by-step:
1. Walk to Cadorna M1 – 7 minutes
2. Take M1 (Red Line) toward Rho Fiera or Bisceglie
3. Change at Lotto to M5 (Lilac Line)
4. Take M5 to San Siro Stadio (last stop)

Travel breakdown:
• M1 ride: ~10 minutes
• Transfer: ~3 minutes
• M5 ride: ~5 minutes
• Walk inside stadium area: ~5–10 minutes
• Security + ticket check: 15–25 minutes (depends on crowd)

✓ Ideal Arrival Time at San Siro: 11:00–11:15
✓ Super easy and the safest route
Pro Tips for San Siro:
  • Bring ID (sometimes required for ticket name checks)
  • No bottles with caps; security removes them
  • Lines for food get VERY long after 11:30 — better to buy early
  • Phone network around the stadium can be slow with many people

Airport Transport (MXP)

From Malpensa → Apartment

BEST OPTION (fastest, easiest, most reliable)
🚆 Malpensa Express → Cadorna Station
Step-by-step:
1. After landing, follow signs: "Malpensa Express → Milano Cadorna"
2. Buy ticket: €13 per person (machines or counter)
3. Board train to Milano Cadorna
4. Ride time: 35–37 minutes
5. Arrive at Milano Cadorna
6. Walk 6–7 minutes to Via Francesco Novati 3
🗺 Walking from Cadorna:
• Exit station on Piazzale Luigi Cadorna
• Walk toward Via Carducci / Corso Magenta
• Turn onto Via Francesco Novati
• Look for number 3

From Apartment → Malpensa

Do exactly the same in reverse:
1. Walk 6–7 minutes to Milano Cadorna
2. Buy ticket: €13 per person
3. Take Malpensa Express (Cadorna → MXP Terminal 1 or 2)
4. Travel time: 35–37 minutes
5. Get off at your terminal (check your flight)
Recommended Timing:
For international flights (like Tel Aviv):
Leave apartment 2.5–3 hours before departure

Why:
• 7 min walk
• 35 min train
• 10 min inside airport
• Security + passport control can be long during holidays
Why This is Perfect for Your Location:
Cadorna is the best station in Milan for Malpensa. Most tourists have to take metro + train, but you only walk 6 minutes and get on the train directly to the airport. This is the easiest, cleanest, fastest airport connection you can get.
